## Risk Assessment Criteria

### Impact
| Level | Definition |
|---|---|
| 5 - Critical | Business continuity impossible, severe legal sanctions |
| 4 - Major | Large-scale operational disruption, loss of key customers |
| 3 - Moderate | Partial business impact, recovery requires significant time |
| 2 - Minor | Limited impact, manageable within normal operations |
| 1 - Negligible | Virtually no impact |

### Likelihood
| Level | Definition |
|---|---|
| 5 - Almost Certain | Almost certain to occur within one year |
| 4 - Likely | High probability of occurring within one year |
| 3 - Possible | May occur within several years |
| 2 - Unlikely | Low probability of occurring |
| 1 - Rare | Occurs only under exceptional circumstances |
