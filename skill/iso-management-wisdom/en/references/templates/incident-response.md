## Initial Response Checklist
- [ ] Recorded an overview of the event
- [ ] Performed a preliminary assessment of the impact scope
- [ ] Reported to the escalation contact
- [ ] Implemented initial containment measures
- [ ] Preserved evidence (logs, screenshots, etc.)
- [ ] Checked legal notification obligations (e.g., personal data breach)
- [ ] Determined whether external reporting is required (regulatory authorities, customers, etc.)
