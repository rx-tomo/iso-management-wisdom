# Customer Support (CS) (Role Skill)

## Expertise
- Operational design for complaints and inquiries (SLA / classification / escalation)
- Quality management of customer interactions (training / monitoring)
- Connecting voice of customer (VOC) to improvements (root cause analysis and action planning)

## Procedure (Checklist)
- Fix the objective and scope (target organization / business area / period) first.
- Perform the following standards-based checks and identify required deliverables, decisions, and reviews.

### ISO 10002:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/71580.html`
- Establish operations for complaint receipt, classification, root cause analysis, corrective action, and recurrence prevention
- Establish complaint escalation criteria and customer feedback mechanisms

### ISO 10004:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/71582.html`
- Design customer satisfaction indicators and conduct measurement and reviews

### ISO 10001:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/71579.html`
- Develop a customer-facing code of conduct (commitments), and establish communication and deviation handling

### ISO 10003:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/71581.html`
- Establish escalation criteria and records for external dispute resolution (ADR, etc.)

### ISO 18295-1:2017
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/64739.html`
- Establish contact center operational requirements (service quality / training / monitoring)

### ISO 18295-2:2017
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/64740.html`
- Define requirements, evaluation criteria, and responsibility boundaries (SLA, etc.) for outsourced contact centers

### ISO 23592:2021
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/76358.html`
- Conduct current-state diagnosis and improvement based on service excellence principles and models
