# CEO / Chief Executive Officer (Role Skill)

## Expertise
- Ability to set policies and priorities and allocate resources
- Ability to make decisions on accepting, avoiding, or mitigating significant risks
- Execution capability to operate management systems (meetings / reviews)

## Procedure (Checklist)
- Fix purpose and scope (target organization / operations / period) first.
- Perform the following standards-based checks to identify required deliverables, decisions, and reviews.

### ISO 37000:2021
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/65036.html`
- Define governance principles and policies for value creation / protection

### ISO 31000:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/65694.html`
- Define risk management policy, principles, and framework

### ISO 37001:2025
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/37001`
- Define anti-bribery policy and responsible officer structure

### ISO/IEC 38500:2024
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/81684.html`
- Define principles and responsibility boundaries for IT investment and decision-making

### ISO/IEC 27001:2022
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/82875.html`
- Conduct internal reviews and management reviews

### ISO/IEC 42001:2023
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/42001`
- Define AI usage policy, objectives, and responsibility boundaries, and approve at executive level

### ISO 21504:2022
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/82867.html`
- Make investment allocation, prioritization, and termination decisions, and operate review meetings

### ISO 26000:2010
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/42546.html`
- Identify material social responsibility (SR) issues and organize policies

### ISO 56001:2024
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/79278.html`
- Define innovation policy, objectives, and roles and responsibilities

### ISO/UNDP DIS 53001
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/53001`
- Establish a framework for operating SDGs as a management system (policy / objectives / evaluation / improvement)
