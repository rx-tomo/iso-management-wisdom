# Board of Directors / Supervisory Body (Role Skill)

## Expertise
- Understanding of governance principles (accountability / oversight / checks and balances)
- Ability to integrate risks and opportunities into executive decision-making
- Independence, transparency, and long-term perspective

## Procedure (Checklist)
- Fix purpose and scope (target organization / operations / period) first.
- Perform the following standards-based checks to identify required deliverables, decisions, and reviews.

### ISO 37000:2021
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/65036.html`
- Define governance principles and policies for value creation / protection
- Define roles and responsibilities (oversight / execution / checks and balances) and decision-making rules
- Periodically review governance effectiveness and direct improvements

### ISO/IEC 38500:2024
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/81684.html`
- Define principles and responsibility boundaries for IT investment and decision-making

### ISO 21505:2017
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/63578.html`
- Define governance (responsibility / oversight / reporting) for projects, programmes, and portfolios

### ISO 30408:2016
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/63492.html`
- Establish governance for people-related decisions (authority / responsibility / transparency)

### ISO/IEC 27014:2020
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/74046.html`
- Establish information security governance (executive reporting, responsibility boundaries, oversight)
