# Chief Information Security Officer (CISO/ISMS) (Role Skill)

## Expertise
- Design controls on a risk basis
- Operate incident response, exercises, and improvement cycles
- Support audits (evidence, SoA, reviews)

## Procedure (Checklist)
- Fix the objective and scope (target organization / business area / period) first.
- Perform the following standards-based checks and identify required deliverables, decisions, and reviews.

### ISO/IEC 38500:2024
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/81684.html`
- Review the impact of IT on value, risk, and conformance

### ISO/IEC 27001:2022
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/82875.html`
- Define information assets and risk assessment procedures, and operate a risk register
- Select and operate controls (SoA / control measures), and maintain evidence
- Operate incident response and improvement (corrective / recurrence prevention)

### ISO/IEC 42001:2023
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/42001`
- Assess AI risks and impacts, and define countermeasures (controls) and monitoring items
- Operate change management and incident response for data, models, and operations

### ISO/IEC 27701:2025
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/27701`
- Establish incident response (notification / recording / corrective action) and audit trails

### ISO/IEC 20000-1:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/70636.html`
- Operate change, release, incident, and problem management

### ISO/IEC 27002:2022
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/75652.html`
- Select information security controls and maintain operational evidence

### ISO/IEC 27005:2022
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/80585.html`
- Define information security risk assessment procedures, acceptance criteria, and review frequency

### ISO/IEC 27014:2020
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/74046.html`
- Establish information security governance (management reporting, responsibility boundaries, oversight)

### ISO/IEC 27035-1:2023
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/78973.html`
- Establish processes for security incident detection, response, and lessons learned (improvement)

### ISO/IEC 27032:2023
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/76070.html`
- Clarify internet security responsibility boundaries and countermeasure areas, and operationalize them

### ISO/IEC 27034-1:2011
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/44378.html`
- Establish an application security framework and integrate it into the development process

### ISO/IEC 27017:2015
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/43757.html`
- Clarify the cloud shared responsibility model and define specific controls

### ISO/IEC 27018:2025
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/27018`
- Establish PII protection in the cloud (controls as a processor)

### ISO/IEC 27036-2:2022
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/82060.html`
- Establish supplier relationship requirements (evaluation / contract / audit / corrective action)

### ISO/IEC 27036-3:2023
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/82890.html`
- Operate supply chain security assessment and management (HW / SW / services)
