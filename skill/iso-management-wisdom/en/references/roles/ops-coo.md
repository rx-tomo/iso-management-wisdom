# COO / Corporate Planning (Role Skill)

## Expertise
- Translate strategy into KPIs and execution plans
- Cross-functional coordination (inter-departmental / outsourcing / stakeholders)
- Portfolio perspective (prioritization / termination decisions)

## Procedure (Checklist)
- Fix the objective and scope (target organization / business area / period) first.
- Perform the following standards-based checks and identify required deliverables, decisions, and reviews.

### ISO 37000:2021
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/65036.html`
- Define roles and responsibilities (oversight / execution / checks and balances) and decision-making rules

### ISO 31000:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/65694.html`
- Define risk criteria (impact / probability scales and acceptance criteria)
- Operate a risk register with periodic reviews and escalation

### ISO 37301:2021
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/75080.html`
- Establish training, communication, and whistleblowing mechanisms

### ISO 19011:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/70017.html`
- Connect findings, corrective actions, and follow-ups to the improvement backlog

### ISO 9001:2015
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/62085.html`
- Define customer requirements and scope of application
- Define processes and KPIs, and perform operational control (including change management)

### ISO 45001:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/63787.html`
- Identify hazards, assess risks, and operate control measures
- Establish worker participation, training, and communication

### ISO 45003:2021
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/64283.html`
- Identify psychosocial risks and operate countermeasures and effectiveness measurement

### ISO/IEC 38500:2024
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/81684.html`
- Review the impact of IT on value, risk, and conformance

### ISO/IEC 42001:2023
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/42001`
- Define AI policy, objectives, and responsibility boundaries, and approve as management

### ISO/IEC 20000-1:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/70636.html`
- Define the service catalogue, SLAs, and roles and responsibilities

### ISO 21502:2020
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/74947.html`
- Clarify objectives, deliverables, and constraints, and develop the plan

### ISO 21503:2022
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/82868.html`
- Manage benefits and dependencies

### ISO 10002:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/71580.html`
- Establish complaint escalation criteria and customer feedback mechanisms

### ISO 30414:2025
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/30414`
- Operate data requirements, quality management, and disclosure (internal / external)

### ISO 30415:2021
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/71164.html`
- Establish D&I policy, objectives, initiatives, and measurement operations

### ISO 26000:2010
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/42546.html`
- Operate stakeholder engagement and accountability (disclosure / dialogue)

### ISO 14064-1:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/66453.html`
- Define organizational GHG quantification boundaries, calculation rules, evidence, and reporting

### ISO 14001:2015
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/60857.html`
- Establish and execute objectives, plans, and operational controls (including change management)

### ISO 22301:2019
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/75106.html`
- Identify critical business functions (BIA) and develop continuity strategies (RTO / RPO, etc.)

### ISO 50001:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/69426.html`
- Conduct energy reviews and define indicators (EnPI) and baselines

### ISO 55001:2024
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/83054.html`
- Establish asset registers and lifecycle plans (renewal / disposal / maintenance)

### ISO 56001:2024
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/79278.html`
- Operate exploration, validation, and implementation portfolio and learning (knowledge capture)

### ISO 30401:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/68683.html`
- Define processes and responsibilities for knowledge identification, creation, sharing, and maintenance
- Operate knowledge updating, inventory, and reuse (documented information)

### ISO 30409:2016
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/64150.html`
- Estimate workforce demand and supply, and plan gap measures (recruitment / development / outsourcing, etc.)

### ISO 22316:2017
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/50053.html`
- Operate organizational resilience self-assessment and review of strengthening measures

### ISO 23592:2021
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/76358.html`
- Conduct current-state diagnosis and improvement based on service excellence principles and models

### ISO 9004:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/70397.html`
- Operate maturity self-assessment and improvement toward sustained organizational success

### ISO 37101:2016
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/61885.html`
- Establish sustainability policy, objectives, operations, evaluation, and improvement as a management system

### ISO/TS 14092:2020
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/68509.html`
- Establish requirements and procedures for local / community adaptation planning

### ISO 14093:2022
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/68511.html`
- Establish the framework for adaptation finance (grants, etc.) requirements and evaluation

### ISO 14097:2021
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/72433.html`
- Establish the framework for climate-related assessment and reporting of financing and investment activities
