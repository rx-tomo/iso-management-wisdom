# Quality Management (QMS) (Role Skill)

## Expertise
- Process design (quality KPIs / corrective action / recurrence prevention)
- Integration of customer requirements and change management
- Continual improvement (CAPA, internal reviews)

## Procedure (Checklist)
- Fix the objective and scope (target organization / business area / period) first.
- Perform the following standards-based checks and identify required deliverables, decisions, and reviews.

### ISO 19011:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/70017.html`
- Develop the audit programme (plan / frequency / scope)

### ISO 9001:2015
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/62085.html`
- Define customer requirements and scope of application
- Define processes and KPIs, and perform operational control (including change management)
- Operate nonconformity corrective action (CAPA) and continual improvement

### ISO 21502:2020
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/74947.html`
- Ensure stakeholder communication and quality assurance

### ISO 10002:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/71580.html`
- Establish operations for complaint receipt, classification, root cause analysis, corrective action, and recurrence prevention

### ISO 10004:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/71582.html`
- Design customer satisfaction indicators and conduct measurement and reviews

### ISO 30401:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/68683.html`
- Define processes and responsibilities for knowledge identification, creation, sharing, and maintenance

### IEC 31010:2019
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/72140.html`
- Select risk assessment techniques and apply them according to the target event

### ISO 10001:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/71579.html`
- Develop a customer-facing code of conduct (commitments), and establish communication and deviation handling

### ISO 18295-1:2017
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/64739.html`
- Establish contact center operational requirements (service quality / training / monitoring)

### ISO 9004:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/70397.html`
- Operate maturity self-assessment and improvement toward sustained organizational success
