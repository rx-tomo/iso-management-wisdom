# Asset Management (AM) (Role Skill)

## Expertise
- Design of asset registers, lifecycle plans, and investment decisions
- Risk and performance monitoring and optimization
- Decision-making and record-keeping for maintenance, renewal, and disposal

## Procedure (Checklist)
- Fix the objective and scope (target organization / business area / period) first.
- Perform the following standards-based checks and identify required deliverables, decisions, and reviews.

### ISO 55001:2024
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/83054.html`
- Establish asset registers and lifecycle plans (renewal / disposal / maintenance)
- Monitor asset performance, risk, and cost, and make investment decisions
