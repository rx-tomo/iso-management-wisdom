# Internal Audit / Internal Review Manager (Role Skill)

## Expertise
- Audit techniques (planning / execution / reporting / corrective action follow-up)
- Independence, objectivity, and evidence-based approach
- Ability to connect findings to an improvement backlog

## Procedure (Checklist)
- Fix purpose and scope (target organization / operations / period) first.
- Perform the following standards-based checks to identify required deliverables, decisions, and reviews.

### ISO 37000:2021
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/65036.html`
- Periodically review governance effectiveness and direct improvements

### ISO 37301:2021
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/75080.html`
- Evaluate effectiveness through monitoring and measurement, and apply corrective actions and improvements

### ISO 19011:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/70017.html`
- Develop an audit programme (plan / frequency / scope)
- Conduct audits (evidence collection, interviews, records)
- Connect findings to corrective actions and follow-up via the improvement backlog

### ISO 9001:2015
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/62085.html`
- Operate nonconformity corrective actions (CAPA) and continual improvement

### ISO 45001:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/63787.html`
- Operate incident / nonconformity reporting, investigation, and corrective actions

### ISO/IEC 27001:2022
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/82875.html`
- Conduct internal reviews and management reviews

### ISO/IEC 20000-1:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/70636.html`
- Drive continual improvement (KPIs, reviews, corrective actions)

### ISO 20400:2017
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/63026.html`
- Operate supplier evaluation and improvement (audits / corrective actions)

### ISO 14001:2015
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/60857.html`
- Achieve continual improvement through monitoring, measurement, internal reviews, and corrective actions

### ISO 22301:2019
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/75106.html`
- Operate event response reviews and corrective actions / improvements

### ISO 50001:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/69426.html`
- Establish measurement data requirements and improvement plans, and drive effectiveness measurement

### ISO 30401:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/68683.html`
- Operate knowledge updates, inventory, and reuse (documented information)

### ISO 37002:2021
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/65035.html`
- Perform intake, triage, investigation, corrective action, and recurrence prevention with records and reviews

### ISO 37003:2025
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/37003`
- Operate fraud incident investigation, corrective actions, and recurrence prevention

### ISO 37302:2025
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/37302`
- Design effectiveness evaluation of compliance measures (KPIs / monitoring and measurement / investigation)

### ISO 14064-3:2019
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/66455.html`
- Prepare evidence trails and corrective processes for verification / validation of GHG statements
