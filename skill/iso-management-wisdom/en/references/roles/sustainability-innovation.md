# Innovation Management (IM) (Role Skill)

## Expertise
- Portfolio management across exploration, validation, and implementation
- Learning mechanisms (lessons from failure) and knowledge capture
- Alignment with business strategy and resource allocation

## Procedure (Checklist)
- Fix the objective and scope (target organization / business area / time period) first.
- Perform the standard-by-standard checks below to identify required deliverables, decisions, and reviews.

### ISO 56001:2024
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/79278.html`
- Define innovation policy, objectives, and roles and responsibilities
- Run portfolio management across exploration, validation, and implementation, and operate learning (knowledge capture)
