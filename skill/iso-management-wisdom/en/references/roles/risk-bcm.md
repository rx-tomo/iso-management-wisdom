# Business Continuity Manager (BCM) (Role Skill)

## Expertise
- Critical business identification (BIA) and RTO/RPO design
- Exercise / drill planning and improvement cycles
- Crisis decision-making and communication

## Procedure (Checklist)
- Fix purpose and scope (target organization / operations / period) first.
- Perform the following standards-based checks to identify required deliverables, decisions, and reviews.

### ISO 22301:2019
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/75106.html`
- Identify critical business operations (BIA) and develop continuity strategies (RTO / RPO, etc.)
- Plan and execute BCPs, recovery procedures, and exercises / drills
- Operate event response reviews and corrective actions / improvements

### ISO 22316:2017
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/50053.html`
- Operate organizational resilience self-assessment and review of strengthening measures
