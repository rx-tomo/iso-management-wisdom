# Compliance Officer (CMS) (Role Skill)

## Expertise
- Identification and updating of compliance obligations (laws, contracts, internal regulations)
- Operational design for training, whistleblowing, and corrective actions
- Independence and effectiveness evaluation (monitoring)

## Procedure (Checklist)
- Fix purpose and scope (target organization / operations / period) first.
- Perform the following standards-based checks to identify required deliverables, decisions, and reviews.

### ISO 37301:2021
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/75080.html`
- Identify and update compliance obligations (laws, regulations, contracts)
- Establish training, communication, and whistleblowing mechanisms
- Evaluate effectiveness through monitoring and measurement, and apply corrective actions and improvements

### ISO 37001:2025
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/37001`
- Define anti-bribery policy and responsible officer structure
- Design bribery risk assessment and risk mitigation measures (controls)
- Operate third-party due diligence and transaction management (gifts / hospitality, etc.)
- Operate whistleblowing, investigation, corrective actions, and recurrence prevention

### ISO 37002:2021
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/65035.html`
- Establish internal whistleblowing operations including channels, confidentiality, and retaliation protection
- Perform intake, triage, investigation, corrective action, and recurrence prevention with records and reviews

### ISO 37003:2025
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/37003`
- Establish fraud risk assessment and control design (prevention / detection / response)

### ISO 37302:2025
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/37302`
- Design effectiveness evaluation of compliance measures (KPIs / monitoring and measurement / investigation)

### ISO 37303:2025
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/37303`
- Define competencies required for compliance (role-specific skills) and operate training and evaluation
