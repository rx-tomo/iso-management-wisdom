# Enterprise Risk Manager (ERM) (Role Skill)

## Expertise
- Risk criteria design (scales / acceptance criteria)
- Risk register operations (review / corrective actions / escalation)
- Enterprise-wide risk integration (business / legal / IT / ESG)

## Procedure (Checklist)
- Fix purpose and scope (target organization / operations / period) first.
- Perform the following standards-based checks to identify required deliverables, decisions, and reviews.

### ISO 31000:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/65694.html`
- Define risk management policy, principles, and framework
- Define risk criteria (impact / likelihood scales and acceptance criteria)
- Operate the risk register with periodic reviews and escalation

### ISO/IEC 27001:2022
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/82875.html`
- Define information asset and risk assessment procedures, and operate the risk register

### ISO/IEC 42001:2023
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/42001`
- Assess AI risks and impacts, and define controls and monitoring items

### ISO 31022:2020
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/69295.html`
- Integrate legal risk identification, assessment, and treatment into risk management

### ISO 55001:2024
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/83054.html`
- Monitor asset performance, risk, and cost, and make investment decisions

### ISO 37003:2025
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/37003`
- Establish fraud risk assessment and control design (prevention / detection / response)

### IEC 31010:2019
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/72140.html`
- Select risk assessment techniques and apply them appropriately to the target events

### ISO/IEC 27005:2022
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/80585.html`
- Define information security risk assessment procedures, acceptance criteria, and review frequency

### ISO 14090:2019
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/68507.html`
- Establish operations (planning / controls) based on climate change adaptation principles and requirements

### ISO 14091:2021
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/68508.html`
- Establish vulnerability, impact, and risk assessment procedures and review operations
