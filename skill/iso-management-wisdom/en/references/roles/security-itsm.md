# IT Service Management (ITSM) (Role Skill)

## Expertise
- IT service SLA, change, and incident operations
- Continual improvement (KPI / review / problem management)
- Bridging DevOps and operations (standardization and flexibility)

## Procedure (Checklist)
- Fix the objective and scope (target organization / business area / period) first.
- Perform the following standards-based checks and identify required deliverables, decisions, and reviews.

### ISO/IEC 27001:2022
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/82875.html`
- Select and operate controls (SoA / control measures), and maintain evidence
- Operate incident response and improvement (corrective / recurrence prevention)

### ISO/IEC 42001:2023
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/42001`
- Operate change management and incident response for data, models, and operations

### ISO/IEC 20000-1:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/70636.html`
- Define the service catalogue, SLAs, and roles and responsibilities
- Operate change, release, incident, and problem management
- Drive continual improvement (KPIs, reviews, corrective actions)

### ISO/IEC 27002:2022
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/75652.html`
- Select information security controls and maintain operational evidence

### ISO/IEC 27035-1:2023
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/78973.html`
- Establish processes for security incident detection, response, and lessons learned (improvement)

### ISO/IEC 27032:2023
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/76070.html`
- Clarify internet security responsibility boundaries and countermeasure areas, and operationalize them

### ISO/IEC 27034-1:2011
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/44378.html`
- Establish an application security framework and integrate it into the development process
