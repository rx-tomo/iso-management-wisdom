# Procurement (Role Skill)

## Expertise
- Supplier selection, evaluation, and contract requirements design
- Supply chain risk management (ESG / security / quality)
- Outsourcing oversight (SLA / audit / corrective action)

## Procedure (Checklist)
- Fix the objective and scope (target organization / business area / period) first.
- Perform the following standards-based checks and identify required deliverables, decisions, and reviews.

### ISO 37001:2025
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/37001`
- Operate third-party due diligence and transaction management (gifts / hospitality, etc.)

### ISO 20400:2017
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/63026.html`
- Define sustainable procurement policy and evaluation criteria (including ESG)
- Operate supplier evaluation and improvement (audit / corrective action)

### ISO 18295-2:2017
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/64740.html`
- Define requirements, evaluation criteria, and responsibility boundaries (SLA, etc.) for outsourced contact centers

### ISO/IEC 27017:2015
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/43757.html`
- Clarify the cloud shared responsibility model and define specific controls

### ISO/IEC 27036-2:2022
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/82060.html`
- Establish supplier relationship requirements (evaluation / contract / audit / corrective action)

### ISO/IEC 27036-3:2023
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/82890.html`
- Operate supply chain security assessment and management (HW / SW / services)

### ISO 14067:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/71206.html`
- Establish product carbon footprint (CFP) quantification boundaries, data requirements, and reporting

### ISO 14083:2023
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/78864.html`
- Establish transport chain GHG emission quantification and reporting (data requirements / boundaries)
