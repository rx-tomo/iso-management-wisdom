# Project Manager (PM) (Role Skill)

## Expertise
- Integrated management of scope, schedule, cost, and quality
- Risk, issue, and change operations (consensus building)
- Transparent reporting (status / forecasts / decisions)

## Procedure (Checklist)
- Fix the objective and scope (target organization / business area / period) first.
- Perform the following standards-based checks and identify required deliverables, decisions, and reviews.

### ISO 21502:2020
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/74947.html`
- Clarify objectives, deliverables, and constraints, and develop the plan
- Operate progress, issues, changes, and risks, and record decisions
- Ensure stakeholder communication and quality assurance

### ISO 22301:2019
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/75106.html`
- Plan and conduct BCP, recovery procedures, and drills / exercises

### ISO 21500:2021
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/75704.html`
- Unify the context and concepts of project, programme, and portfolio, and define scope of application

### ISO 21506:2024
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/87900.html`
- Define PM terminology and maintain consistency across documents, meetings, and reports

### ISO 21508:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/63582.html`
- Define earned value management (EVM) baselines and metrics, and operate reporting

### ISO 21511:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/69702.html`
- Use WBS for work decomposition, responsibility assignment, and estimation / planning foundations

### ISO 21512:2024
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/63584.html`
- Define EVM implementation procedures (roles / frequency / reports) and support adoption

### ISO 14064-2:2019
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/66454.html`
- Establish project-level GHG reduction / removal quantification, monitoring, and reporting
