# ESG / Sustainability (Role Skill)

## Expertise
- Operational design from ESG policy to objectives, execution, and evaluation
- Understanding of GHG quantification, claims, and verification requirements
- Integration into procurement, financing, and business operations

## Procedure (Checklist)
- Fix the objective and scope (target organization / business area / time period) first.
- Perform the standard-by-standard checks below to identify required deliverables, decisions, and reviews.

### ISO 20400:2017
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/63026.html`
- Define sustainable procurement policy and evaluation criteria (including ESG)

### ISO 26000:2010
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/42546.html`
- Identify social responsibility (SR) material issues (materiality) and organize policies
- Operate stakeholder engagement and accountability (disclosure / dialogue)

### ISO 14064-1:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/66453.html`
- Define the organization's GHG quantification boundary, calculation rules, evidence, and reporting

### ISO 14068-1:2023
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/43279.html`
- Establish conditions and evidence for carbon neutrality / net-zero claims

### ISO 37101:2016
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/61885.html`
- Establish sustainability policy, objectives, operations, evaluation, and improvement as a management system

### ISO 14064-2:2019
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/66454.html`
- Establish project-level GHG emission reduction / removal quantification, monitoring, and reporting

### ISO 14064-3:2019
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/66455.html`
- Prepare evidence and corrective processes for verification / validation of GHG statements

### ISO 14067:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/71206.html`
- Establish product carbon footprint (CFP) quantification boundary, data requirements, and reporting

### ISO 14083:2023
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/78864.html`
- Establish GHG emission quantification and reporting for transport chains (data requirements / boundaries)

### ISO 14090:2019
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/68507.html`
- Establish climate change adaptation operations (planning / controls) based on principles and requirements

### ISO 14091:2021
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/68508.html`
- Establish vulnerability, impact, and risk assessment procedures and review operations

### ISO/TS 14092:2020
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/68509.html`
- Establish requirements and procedures for local / community adaptation planning

### ISO 14093:2022
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/68511.html`
- Establish the framework for adaptation finance (grants, etc.) requirements and evaluation

### ISO 14097:2021
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/72433.html`
- Establish the framework for climate-related assessment and reporting of financing activities

### ISO/UNDP DIS 53001
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/53001`
- Establish the framework for operating SDGs as a management system (policy / objectives / evaluation / improvement)
