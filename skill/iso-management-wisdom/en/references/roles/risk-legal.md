# Chief Legal Officer (Role Skill)

## Expertise
- Risk judgment and advisory on contracts, regulations, and disputes
- Understanding of the importance of fact-finding, record-keeping, and evidence management
- Internal coordination (business / compliance / information security)

## Procedure (Checklist)
- Fix purpose and scope (target organization / operations / period) first.
- Perform the following standards-based checks to identify required deliverables, decisions, and reviews.

### ISO 37301:2021
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/75080.html`
- Identify and update compliance obligations (laws, regulations, contracts)

### ISO 37001:2025
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/37001`
- Design bribery risk assessment and risk mitigation measures (controls)
- Operate whistleblowing, investigation, corrective actions, and recurrence prevention

### ISO/IEC 27701:2025
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/27701`
- Operate personal information processing inventories, DPIAs, and processor management

### ISO 31022:2020
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/69295.html`
- Integrate legal risk identification, assessment, and treatment into risk management

### ISO 14068-1:2023
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/43279.html`
- Establish conditions and evidence trails for carbon neutrality / net-zero claims

### ISO 14001:2015
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/60857.html`
- Identify and manage environmental aspects and compliance obligations (laws, contracts, etc.)

### ISO 37002:2021
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/65035.html`
- Establish internal whistleblowing operations including channels, confidentiality, and retaliation protection

### ISO 37003:2025
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/37003`
- Operate fraud incident investigation, corrective actions, and recurrence prevention

### ISO 10003:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/71581.html`
- Establish escalation criteria and records for external dispute resolution (ADR, etc.)
