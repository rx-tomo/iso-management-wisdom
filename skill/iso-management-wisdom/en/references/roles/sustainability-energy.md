# Energy Management (EnMS) (Role Skill)

## Expertise
- Energy review and indicator (EnPI) design
- Data measurement, baseline management, and improvement effectiveness measurement
- Planning and execution management of energy-saving measures

## Procedure (Checklist)
- Fix the objective and scope (target organization / business area / time period) first.
- Perform the standard-by-standard checks below to identify required deliverables, decisions, and reviews.

### ISO 50001:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/69426.html`
- Conduct an energy review and define indicators (EnPI) and baselines
- Establish measurement data requirements and improvement plans, and run effectiveness measurement
