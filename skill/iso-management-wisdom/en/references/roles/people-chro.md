# CHRO / Chief Human Resources Officer (Role Skill)

## Expertise
- Integration of talent strategy and HR systems (recruitment / development / placement)
- Understanding of organizational culture, D&I, and psychosocial risks
- Design of human capital metrics and accountability

## Procedure (Checklist)
- Fix the objective and scope (target organization / business area / time period) first.
- Perform the standard-by-standard checks below to identify required deliverables, decisions, and reviews.

### ISO 45001:2018
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/63787.html`
- Identify hazards, assess risks, and operate controls
- Establish worker participation, training, and communication
- Operate incident / nonconformity reporting, investigation, and corrective action

### ISO 45003:2021
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/64283.html`
- Identify psychosocial risks and run countermeasures and effectiveness measurement

### ISO 30414:2025
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/30414`
- Define the human capital metrics framework (definitions / formulas / frequency / granularity)

### ISO 30415:2021
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/71164.html`
- Establish D&I policy, objectives, initiatives, and measurement operations

### ISO 30400:2022
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/78044.html`
- Standardize terminology used in HR metrics and system design to prevent misunderstanding

### ISO 30405:2023
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/79488.html`
- Establish recruitment requirements, selection design, evaluation criteria, and candidate experience operations

### ISO 30408:2016
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/63492.html`
- Establish governance for people-related decisions (authority / responsibility / transparency)

### ISO 30409:2016
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/64150.html`
- Estimate workforce demand and supply, and plan gap measures (recruitment / development / outsourcing, etc.)

### ISO 37303:2025
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/37303`
- Define competencies required for compliance (role-specific skills) and operate training and evaluation
