# Environmental Management (EMS) (Role Skill)

## Expertise
- Identification and operational control of environmental aspects and compliance obligations
- Operation of objectives, plans, monitoring and measurement, and corrective action
- External disclosure / audit readiness (preparation of evidence)

## Procedure (Checklist)
- Fix the objective and scope (target organization / business area / time period) first.
- Perform the standard-by-standard checks below to identify required deliverables, decisions, and reviews.

### ISO 14001:2015
- Reference: `https://www.iso.org/standard/60857.html`
- Identify and manage environmental aspects and compliance obligations (laws, contracts, etc.)
- Establish and execute objectives, plans, and operational controls (including change management)
- Achieve continual improvement through monitoring and measurement, internal reviews, and corrective action
